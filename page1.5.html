Why is reading important?

Reading for pleasure has been shown to improve text comprehension, grammar, and vocabulary. It's even more important for a child's educational success than their family's socio-economic status (OECD, 2002; Clark and Rumbold, 2006)

Regular reading habits can also enhance critical thinking skills and expand one's worldview through exposure to diverse perspectives and ideas.

Key Benefits

  • Increase your vocabulary and comprehension skills
  • Reduce stress
  • Improve mental health
  • Improve memory and concentration
  • Encourage lifelong learning
  • Provide entertainment and escape
